Our Voyage

Welcome to Yachts Zenith, a beacon of luxury yachting, originated in the vibrant heart of London, alongside the historic Thames. Founded by Julian Pirvu, a man deeply passionate about the sea, Yachts Zenith represents more than a charter service – it's a gateway to the majestic world of yachting accessible to all. Driven by a commitment to excellence and discretion, Julian has meticulously curated a fleet of elegant, high-performance yachts. These vessels are not just chosen for their aesthetic appeal but for the unparalleled experiences they offer.

At Yachts Zenith, every journey is custom-crafted. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a lavish celebration, each voyage is a testament to our philosophy: yachting is not just a pastime, it's an inclusive adventure. Renowned for our exceptional service and a deep respect for privacy, Yachts Zenith has attracted a discerning international clientele. They are drawn to our bespoke experiences and our dedication to maintaining the utmost discretion.
