Our Voyage

In London's vibrant heart, by the historic Thames, Yachts Zenith began a quest to redefine luxury yachting. Julian Pirvu, driven by a deep-seated passion for the sea, founded more than just a charter service; he created a gateway to make the elegance of yachting accessible for everyone. Starting humbly, Julian established a service founded on excellence and discretion, selecting a fleet of elegant, high-performance yachts not merely for their aesthetic appeal, but for the extraordinary experiences they could provide. Every voyage with Yachts Zenith, whether a cosy gathering or a lavish celebration, is uniquely tailored to its guests. The company's philosophy was straightforward: yachting should be an exhilarating, accessible adventure. This vision challenged the traditional exclusivity of yachting, welcoming both seasoned and first-time enthusiasts. As Yachts Zenith's reputation for exemplary service and privacy grew, it attracted a global clientele, drawn to its bespoke experiences and commitment to discretion. Today, with bases in London and Ibiza, Yachts Zenith stands as a paragon of maritime distinction. True to its origins, the company continues to offer unforgettable journeys, earn its guests' trust, and pioneer new frontiers in luxurious, discreet sea travel.
