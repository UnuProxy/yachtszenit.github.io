General Data Policy Information

This Privacy Policy provides an overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit our website. "Personal data" refers to all data that can personally identify you. For detailed information on data protection, please see our Data Protection Declaration below.

Data Recording on Our Website

Who is responsible for data recording on this website?

The data on this website is processed by Yachts Zenith, whose contact details are provided in the section “Information about the Responsible Party” in this Privacy Policy.

How do we record your data?

Your data is collected as a result of your sharing information with us, such as entering data in our contact form. Other data is automatically recorded by our IT systems when you visit the website, which includes technical information like your web browser, operating system, or the time of your visit.

What do we use your data for?

Part of the data is collected to ensure error-free provision of the website. Other data can be used to analyse your user patterns.

What rights do you have regarding your data?

You have the right to receive information about the source, recipients, and purposes of your archived personal data at any time. You also have the right to demand rectification or eradication of your data. Please contact us at any time if you have questions about data protection.

Analysis Tools and Third-Party Tools

Your browsing patterns may be statistically analysed when you visit our website, primarily using analysis programmes. Detailed information about these programmes is provided in our Data Protection Declaration.
