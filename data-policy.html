Welcome to Our Privacy Policy

At Yachts Zenith, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy details the handling of your personal data upon visiting our website. 'Personal data' includes any information that can identify you personally. For an extensive description, please refer to our Data Protection Declaration further below.

Data Collection and Management

Responsible Entity

Data on this website is managed and processed by Yachts Zenith. For our contact information and our responsibilities regarding data processing, please see the “Contact for Data Protection” section of this policy.

Methods of Data Collection

We gather data directly from you, for instance, when you complete our contact form, and automatically via our IT systems, which record details like your browser type, operating system, and the timings of your visits.

Purposes of Data Collection

Our data collection serves two principal aims: to ensure our website functions seamlessly and to analyse user interaction to enhance your experience.

Your Rights Concerning Data

You have the right to enquire about the origin, recipients, and the purpose of your stored data at any time. Moreover, you can request adjustments or deletion of your data. For any queries regarding data protection, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Analysis and Third-Party Tools

We utilise various analytical tools to statistically assess your browsing behaviour on our site. Comprehensive details about these tools are provided in our Data Protection Declaration.

Data Protection Declaration

Categories of Data

We collect a range of data to provide and enhance our services, encompassing personal identification information (such as names and email addresses), usage data (like access times and pages visited), and technical data (including browser types and operating systems).

Objectives and Legal Bases for Processing

We process your data for specific objectives, including responding to enquiries, improving our services, and fulfilling legal requirements. Our processing activities are founded on Articles 6 and 9 of the GDPR.

Data Retention and Eradication

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the outlined objectives, in accordance with legal stipulations. Upon request, we will eradicate your data in compliance with legal provisions.

Ensuring Data Security

To safeguard your data, we employ various measures, such as encryption and secure server hosting. However, it is important to note that no transmission over the Internet is entirely secure.

Your Rights as a Data Subject

You possess rights regarding access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, and data portability. Additionally, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time and to lodge complaints with a supervisory authority.

Data Sharing and International Transfers

Data is shared with third parties only when necessary for service provision, legal compliance, or with your explicit consent. Certain data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area.

Contact for Data Protection Queries

For any enquiries related to data protection, please contact us at info@yachtszenith.com.
